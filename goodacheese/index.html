About Us

Founding:

Gooda Cheese was founded in 1872, by Nathan Hatcher in Edwinton, Dakota. The goal was to make the fanciest, curdled dairy product in the world.
From humble but ambitious beginnings as a small local Cheese dealer, Gooda Cheese has always tried to find ways to innovate. With only pennies to my name I had to improvise ingredients many would consider essential for making Cheese the "Proper" way. Through these circumstances I, Nathan, created new recipes unmatched by any other Cheese "manufacturer" at the time.
Business was slow at the start I remember it well, the stares, the estranged glances my way. Even my family thought it a foolish endeavour. But I persisted through it all, slowly, painfully, gaining a small loyal clientele.

Founder:

I, Nathan was 26 years old at the time Gooda Cheese was formed. I nor my family were ever wealthy, often having forced into eating the creative meals of cobbled together leftovers. My family consisted of my mother, my father, my two younger siblings, and myself. I was always the one to help mother in the kitchen.

Different Owners:

Gooda Cheese is proud to say ownership has never changed hands. It would be daft to consider any other being able to lead Gooda the way I do.

Workplace Safety:

Gooda Cheese takes employee safety very seriously, and have repeatedly responded to employees falling in vats within 20 minutes of the incident. All employees are required to wear plastic hoof coverings and construction grade hard hats. And are trained in proper procedure in case of emergency scenarios.

Shadey allegations:

All false, I don't know why people keep saying that Gooda Cheese is a "suspicious" company.
